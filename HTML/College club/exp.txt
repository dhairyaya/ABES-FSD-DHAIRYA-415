Exercise: Build a 3-Page “College Club” Website (Links, Images, Anchors)

Objective

Practice multi-page navigation, relative links, images with alt text, lists, tables (rowspan/colspan), and in-page anchors—all without CSS.

Deliverables

A folder college-club/ containing:

index.html (Home)
events.html (Events & schedule table)
contact.html (Contact page with mailto/tel links)
images/ (at least 2 images used on pages)

Requirements

1. A navigation bar on every page linking the three pages (use the same snippet).
2. At least two images with meaningful alt.
3. One in-page anchor (e.g., “Jump to Upcoming Events”).
4. One table using thead/tbody and one use of rowspan or colspan.
5. One external link (opens in new tab via target="_blank").
6. One mailto: and one tel: link on Contact page.
